This is the very first post on The License blog. If you're reading this, you're early. The coffee's still brewing.

## Why a blog?

Because a license is more than a block of legalese. It's a statement about how you want to share your work — and what you want back. We want a place to talk about that: the philosophy, the trade-offs, the edge cases, and the stuff we got wrong.

Expect posts about:

- **Source-available vs open source** — what's the actual difference, and when does it matter?
- **The entropy problem** — why communal resources degrade and what to do about it
- **License design** — why certain clauses exist, what we've learned writing them
- **Community stories** — people using the license, adapting it, or telling us we're wrong

## Say hi

If you want to be featured on the blog — maybe you're using the license, you have a take, or you're building something interesting — join the [Discord](https://discord.awfixer.me) and ask. We'd love to hear from you.

The floor is yours. Be interesting, be kind, or be both.
